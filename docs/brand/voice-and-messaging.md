# Voice and Messaging

## Voice

Pool Conductor sounds like a good pool professional explaining a well-designed
system at the equipment pad: clear, relaxed, knowledgeable, and focused on what
matters.

### Plain

Use the words people use around pools. Say `pump speed`, `water temperature`,
`pool light`, and `last seen`. Do not turn simple functions into platform
language.

### Calm

The product exists to remove attention from pool maintenance. Copy should not
manufacture urgency or make every feature sound revolutionary.

### Specific

Trust comes from concrete details. Prefer `control your variable-speed pump in
Apple Home` over `unlock smarter pool experiences`.

### Friendly

Write from builder to builder and neighbor to neighbor. The brand can have a
dry sense of humor, but it should never be cute at the expense of clarity.

### Confident

Use direct sentences. Do not pile qualifiers onto claims the product can plainly
support.

## Message Order

Use this order when introducing the product:

1. The pool asks for less attention.
2. Existing equipment becomes easier to control.
3. Pool Conductor coordinates the pump, relays, and optional sensors.
4. Matter brings those controls into familiar smart-home platforms.
5. Local operation means no required cloud account or subscription.
6. Open-source firmware gives builders another path.

## Message Pillars

### Ready Without the Routine

Pool Conductor handles the ordinary equipment coordination that otherwise sends
someone back to a timer, pump panel, or separate app.

Useful phrases:

- Your pool, ready when you are.
- Less equipment to think about.
- Spend time in the pool, not at the equipment pad.

### One Calm View

The product brings separate pieces of equipment into one understandable system.

Useful phrases:

- Pump, equipment, and pool conditions in one place.
- See what is running without walking outside.
- Simple controls for the things you use most.

### Built for the Equipment You Have

Pool Conductor should feel like a practical upgrade, not a demand to start over.

Useful phrases:

- Bring existing pool equipment into Matter.
- Add modern control without replacing a working pad.
- Six relay channels and optional sensor inputs.

### Better Handoffs

Pool professionals can give customers a system that is easy to use while
retaining the information needed to support it.

Useful phrases:

- Easy for customers to use. Easier for you to support.
- Finish the job with controls the customer already understands.
- Know more before the next service visit.

## Words to Prefer

- control
- local
- connected equipment

- pool professional
- current status
- optional sensors

## Words to Avoid

- revolutionary
- effortless
- magical
- ecosystem
- smart lifestyle
- cutting-edge
- seamless
- disrupt
- ultimate
- AI-powered
- set it and forget it
